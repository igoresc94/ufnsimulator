This Simulator was coded by MA Igor Carnaúba to teach Disinformation in EU Politics through AI to bachelor students at Eötvös Loránd University in Budapest.

By writing a country, disinformation technique, text and uploading an image, students write their disinformation/tacking disinformation post and and everybody will be able to see the way they are working with their countries.

Feel free to check it out and check how chairs can be changed as they act.
